Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is important to communication
  • Most chameleons have a tail used to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels can survive very long periods of time without water
  • They have been used to carry goods across the desert
  • They have a big hump on their back

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas are very endangered
  • They eat a lot of food
  • They live mostly in China

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas are actually harmless
  • They are mostly beneficial
  • They are eaten by almost everything with an insectivorous diet

Interesting Facts about Bears

  • Bears are mammals just like humans
  • Bears grieve deeply for others
  • Bears have excellent sight, hearing, and smell
